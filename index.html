My Interests

Android Development

An android developer designs software and applications for Android devices and the Google Play Store. This software developer creates apps used on Android phones and tablets. They often work for large organizations or directly for an app development company.

Flutter

Flutter is a mobile app development platform created by Google. It allows developers to create web, desktop, and cross-platform apps that run on Android and iOS devices. Flutter uses a reactive programming language called Dart, making development faster and easier than traditional methods.

Blockchain Development

Blockchain development is the process of creating shared, unchangeable, distributed ledger technology (DLT) that securely records transactions and tracks assets—whether those are physical assets, like money or real estate, or nonphysical assets, like copyrights—within a network.
